# Introduction to Networking



## Overview
Networking is the practice of connecting computers and other devices to share information and resources.  
It is fundamental for communication, collaboration, and accessing services in modern organizations and daily life.

## Importance
- Enables communication between devices across local and global networks.
- Supports business operations, cloud computing, and Internet access.
- Critical for modern applications, IoT devices, and online services.

## Common Use Cases
- **Home networks**: connecting computers, phones, smart devices, and printers.
- **Office networks**: enabling collaboration, file sharing, and email services.
- **Data centers**: hosting websites, cloud applications, and databases.
- **Internet communication**: browsing, video calls, and online gaming.
- **IoT (Internet of Things)**: smart devices communicating and automating tasks.
